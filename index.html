About Me

Where to even begin?
I was born in a blink and miss it town in Texas called Caddo Mills. You know how they say everything's always bigger in Texas? As it turns out, the one exception are its cities.
In 2006, during my junior year of high school, my family uprooted and moved to St. Louis and I've been here ever since.
As a kid, I always had a passion for creating things and needing to know how they work, which is where my interest in coding began to develop.
After graduating from high school, I had two passions that I wished to pursue - film-making and coding - but I only had time for one. I chose film. Unfortunately, after spending far too much time, energy, and money, I realized that film-making wasn't for me, but by this time, I had bills to pay and instead of continuing my education and redirecting my career path, I chose to double down at the job where I still work today: NAPA Auto Parts
Now, years later, I'm attempting to right the wrong that I made when I was a kid and begin my new career in the tech industry.
